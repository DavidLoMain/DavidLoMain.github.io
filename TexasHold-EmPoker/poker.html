Overview

Texas Hold-em is a form of Poker where each player is dealt two cards and all players share five community cards. Players make the best five card hand they can using their two cards and the five community cards.

This project will work with a modified version of this game, Crazy Hold-Em, created by Professor Wiegley at California State University, Northridge. Players will be dealt three cards instead of two and Players make the best six card hand they can rather than five card hand.
